# Time Ivy

Time tracking software for hourly billing.

## Time Formats

Time can be entered into the system using a wide variety of formats, such as:

---       | ---
1:00 pm   | 01:00 PM
1:00 p.m. | 01:00 PM
1:00 p    | 01:00 PM
1:00pm    | 01:00 PM
1:00p.m.  | 01:00 PM
1:00p     | 01:00 PM
1 pm      | 01:00 PM
1 p.m.    | 01:00 PM
1 p       | 01:00 PM
1pm       | 01:00 PM
1p.m.     | 01:00 PM
1p        | 01:00 PM
13:00     | 01:00 PM
13        | 01:00 PM
12        | 12:00 PM
2400      | 00:00 AM
1a        | 01:00 AM
100       | 01:00 AM
123       | 01:23 AM
1000      | 10:00 AM
